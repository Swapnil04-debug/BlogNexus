About BlogNexus

BlogNexus is a dynamic blogging platform designed to empower users to share insightful content, manage posts, and explore categorized topics. It offers a smooth, responsive experience across devices.

Key Features

  • User Authentication (Signup, Login, Logout)
  • CRUD posts with image uploads
  • Category filtering (Technology, Health, Travel, Finance, Lifestyle)
  • Pagination for home feed
  • CSRF protection on all forms
  • Responsive, dark-themed UI
